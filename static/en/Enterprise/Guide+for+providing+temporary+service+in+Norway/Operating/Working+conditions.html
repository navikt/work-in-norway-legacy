Requirements for a safe working environment

The Working Environment Act and associated regulations generally apply to all working conditions in Norway. For example, all employees in Norway have the right to:

  • A written contract of employment
  • A safe working environment
  • Regular working hours
  • Holidays
  • A minimum wage in certain industries

General information about working conditions in Norway

If the worker is employed by a foreign enterprise and posted to Norway, special regulations apply. These provisions are intended to secure all workers certain minimum rights in connection with work across international borders.
