Educational system

All children who live in Norway must complete primary and lower secondary education. If you have a child aged 6-15, you must contact the primary or lower secondary school in the municipal authority in which you live.

Banking services

If you are employed in Norway you need a Norwegian bank account to make it easier to receive your pay.

Language tuition for Nordic area citizens

Nordic area citizens are not obliged to take a Norwegian language course. If you do not speak Norwegian, or any other scandinavian language, you should consider to take a Norwegian language course. This course is not free.

Norwegian society

If you want to learn more about Norwegian society and culture, you will get a brief introduction at nyinorge.no. This site also provides useful and practical information, that will help you to get settled in Norway
