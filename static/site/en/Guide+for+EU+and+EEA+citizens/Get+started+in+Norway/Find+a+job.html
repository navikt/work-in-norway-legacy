1.Searching for a job

  • Search for jobs in Norway

2.Recognition of foreign education and regulated professions

If you have education from outside of Norway, read more about how to have your education recognised in Norway. Many professions are regulated by law and require a specific authorisation or licence to practice in Norway. Check the website of the Norwegian Directorate for Higher Education and Skills.

!Corona and travel quarantine

The travel-restrictions upon entry into Norway have been lifted. The same rules as prior to the corona pandemic now apply.

!Working conditions

You can find information about employment contracts, pay, working hours, working environment and holiday at the Norwegian Labour Inspection Authority's website.

!NAV's English job adverts

The jobs database on nav.no is available in Norwegian only, but you can search for job adverts in English by simply searching for the word "English".
