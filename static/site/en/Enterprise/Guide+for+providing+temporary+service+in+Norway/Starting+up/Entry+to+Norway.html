Entry to Norway for citizens of countries outside the EU/EEA

Employees from countries outside the EU/EEA must apply for a residence permit. There are special provisions for posted workers.

Entry to Norway for citizens of EU/EEA countries

Employees from EU/EEA countries must register with the police if they are staying in Norway for more than three months. There are special provisions for posted workers.
