Entry to Norway for citizens of countries outside the EU/EEA

Employees from countries outside the EU/EEA must obtain a residence permit before they can gain entry to Norway. There are special provisions for posted workers.

Entry to Norway for citizens of EU/EEA countries

Employees from EU/EEA countries must register before they begin working in Norway. There are special provisions for posted workers.
