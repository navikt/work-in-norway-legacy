Everyone should be able to use our websites.

Our services must be available to as many people as possible, regardless of functional ability or usage situation.

Contact us about technical errors or deficiencies with Work in Norway

We appreciate your feedback. Send us a message if you find that something on workinnorway.no does not work as it should or is difficult to understand. Report technical errors and deficiencies with workinnorway.no.

Declaration of accessibility

An accessibility statement is a report that shows whether our websites meet the statutory WCAG success criteria. We see the legal requirements as a minimum obligation to make our services available. We work not only to achieve compliance with the legal requirements, but for a good user experience for everyone. See the accessibility statement for workinnorway.no (text in Norwegian).
